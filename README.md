# Amin Hocine, PhD 👋

**Assistant Professor (RTD-A)** · Department of Management, University of Bergamo, Italy
**Founder & Director** of [Xorium Lab](https://xorium.org)

I am a researcher in **Operations Research** and **Decision Sciences**, working at the
intersection of theory and practice. My work develops methods for **decision-making
under uncertainty**, with a particular focus on **Xorness** — a new framework for
uncertainty arising from *indeterminacy, exclusivity, and hesitancy*.

---

### 🔬 Research interests

- **Multi-Criteria Decision Making** (MCDM / MCDA)
- **Multi-objective optimization** & goal programming
- **Optimization under uncertainty** — fuzzy, stochastic, interval, and XOR programming
- **Applications** — renewable energy, sustainability, risk assessment, and cybersecurity

---

### 🧩 The Xorness framework

I introduced the concept of **XOR numbers** and developed a family of XOR-based
decision-making methods that preserve uncertainty throughout the entire decision process:

`XOR-AHP` · `XOR-ANP` · `XOR-DEA` · `XOR-BWM` · `XOR-WSM`

> *Xorness emerges from a state of indeterminacy, where multiple mutually exclusive
> possibilities compete for our attention, yet none is strong enough to dominate.*
